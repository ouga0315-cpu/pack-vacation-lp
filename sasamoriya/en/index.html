A private space
in the heart of the forest

Minshuku Sasamorihouse is a private whole-house rental nestled in the forest of Azumino Gakushamura.

Enjoy a 100㎡, two-story traditional Japanese house entirely to yourself. A 100-inch home theater, an open-plan living room, and a private bathroom newly built in 2025. The expansive 750㎡ garden is perfect for BBQ. Pets are more than welcome.

The stillness here is extraordinary. All you hear is the sway of trees, birdsong, and the faint sound of a distant train. A complete escape from everyday life.

Alongside running the accommodations, he's passionate about sharing Azumino's nature and culture through social media and guiding hiking and sightseeing tours.
