WELCOME

A Stay Where Pets Are Family

Sasamorihouse welcomes guests traveling with pets.

RULES

House Rules for Pet Guests

A few requests so the next guest — and your pet — can both enjoy the house comfortably.

Please do not allow pets on the beds or futons.
Out of consideration for guests with allergies, we ask that pets stay off bedding. Floors, sofas, the entryway, the garden, and other living spaces are all fine.

Other Requests

  • Leash or crate use is recommended indoors
  • Please feed pets in the entryway or kitchen tile area
  • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets, including any accidents
  • For pets that bark often at night, please use a crate or separate room overnight
  • We assume your pet is up to date on rabies and standard vaccinations

WALKS

Walking Routes Nearby

The surroundings make for excellent walks — from quiet villa lanes right outside the door to forest paths in the national park.

Gakushamura Villa Lanes

The villa area around the house has very little traffic, plenty of shade, and a peaceful atmosphere — perfect for morning and evening walks.

Chubu-Sangaku National Park

Forest trails at the foot of the Northern Alps. Cool, shaded paths well suited to walking with your dog.

Azumino Alps National Government Park

Wide lawns and well-maintained walking paths. Pets are welcome on a leash — please check the park's official rules before your visit.
