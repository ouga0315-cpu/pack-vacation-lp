ABOUT / THE GUESTHOUSE

A Casual Guesthouse
for Travelers

Guesthouse Tomoshibi is a quiet, welcoming space set in a beautifully renovated 55-year-old wooden house at the foot of the Northern Alps.

The warm wooden interiors and soft lighting create a peaceful atmosphere. All guests enjoy free access to our shared onsen bath. The shared kitchen and lounge are perfect for winding down after a day of exploring.

Host Ouga welcomes you personally and loves helping guests discover the best of Azumino, from hiking tips to local recommendations.

Whether you're here to climb, hike, or simply enjoy the quiet mountain life, this is your home away from home.

  • Shared onsen bath included / Free for all guests
  • Loved by returning guests
  • Bilingual host (English & Japanese)
  • Full kitchen & shared lounge
  • Perfect base for hiking & mountain activities

YOUR HOST

Meet Ouga Sanjo

Ouga Sanjo

Founder, Pack Vacation

Born and raised in Tokyo, Ouga moved to Azumino at 23 and founded Minshuku Sasamorihouse, a whole-house rental. Since then, he has welcomed hundreds of guests from around the world. Later, he created Tomoshibi to expand his vision of sharing the beauty of mountain life with more travelers.

Beyond hosting, Ouga is passionate about sharing Azumino's natural wonders and culture. He offers hiking guides, local recommendations, and personalized advice to help you experience the best of the region.

Ouga speaks English fluently and loves helping guests create unforgettable memories in Azumino.
