Cancellation Policy

The following cancellation fees apply to reservations. Please notify us as early as possible.

Cancellation Fees

When cancelledFee
8 or more days before check-inFree
7–3 days before check-in30% of total
2–1 days before check-in50% of total
Day of check-in80% of total
No-show100% of total

How to Cancel

  1. Click the "Cancel reservation" link in your confirmation email (auto-processed up to 48 hours before check-in).
  2. Within 48 hours of check-in, please contact us directly via Instagram DM or phone.

Force Majeure

If natural disasters (earthquake, typhoon, heavy snow), pandemic outbreaks, or similar circumstances beyond your control prevent your stay, cancellation fees may be waived or reduced. Please contact us as soon as possible.

Cancellation by the Inn

If we cannot provide accommodation due to facility issues, natural disasters, or host illness, we will refund 100% of the booking amount.

Changes (Dates, Number of Guests)

Free changes are accepted up to 8 days before check-in. Later changes may be treated as new bookings and incur the cancellation fees above.
