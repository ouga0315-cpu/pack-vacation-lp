Privacy Policy

Pack Vacation ("the Inn") handles personal information in accordance with the following policy.

1. Information We Collect

  1. Name, address, phone number, email, occupation
  2. Nationality and passport number (non-Japanese guests)
  3. Booking details: dates, number of guests, property, requests
  4. Website access logs (IP address, browser info, cookies)

2. Purpose of Use

  1. Processing reservations, confirmations, modifications, cancellations
  2. Providing accommodation services and day-of communication
  3. Maintaining the guest registry as required by Japanese Inn Business Act (3 years)
  4. Responding to inquiries and handling issues
  5. Statistical analysis to improve services (anonymized)

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  1. With your consent
  2. When required by law (including legitimate requests from police or government agencies)
  3. When necessary to protect life, body, or property

4. Subprocessors

We use Google Workspace (Apps Script, Gmail, Sheets, Calendar) for parts of our booking and notification systems. These services are managed under Google LLC's privacy policy.

5. Retention Period

  1. Guest registry: 3 years (as required by Japanese Inn Business Act)
  2. Other booking data: deleted promptly after the purpose is fulfilled

6. Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or leakage of personal information.

7. Your Rights

You may request disclosure, correction, or deletion of your personal information. We will respond reasonably after verifying your identity. Please contact us at the address below.
